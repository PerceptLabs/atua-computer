## What Real Syscall Coverage Requires

1. A real Linux execution engine that makes real syscall invocations
2. A tracing mechanism that captures actual syscall usage during workload execution
3. Comparison of traced syscalls against the must-have and should-have lists defined in the specs
4. Identification of genuinely missing or broken syscalls
5. Iterative implementation of missing syscalls until coverage targets are met

Syscall gap analysis cannot produce meaningful results until at minimum Phase B (Engine Bring-up) is complete with a real engine.
